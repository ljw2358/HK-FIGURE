1. Executive Overview (English)

HK-FIGURE is preparing a transformative industrial strategy centered on the acquisition of POSCO, one of the world's most respected steel producers. This strategic move aims to redefine advanced manufacturing, energy infrastructure, robotics, quantum materials, and sustainable industrial ecosystems.

By integrating POSCO’s advanced metallurgical capabilities with HK-FIGURE’s innovation-driven ecosystem in AI, robotics, fusion energy systems, space engineering, bio-material science, and digital banking infrastructure, the combined entity will become a vertically integrated global powerhouse.

The acquisition will enable synergy across raw materials, smart manufacturing, energy transition metals, green hydrogen infrastructure, smart cities, and next-generation aerospace components.

HK-FIGURE envisions a new industrial renaissance powered by sustainable steel, AI automation, quantum-enhanced materials research, and zero-carbon energy systems.

Strategic Pillars:

  • Advanced Materials & Quantum Metallurgy
  • Green Hydrogen & Fusion Infrastructure
  • Robotics & Automated Smart Factories
  • Space & Aerospace Structural Components
  • Digital Finance & Industrial Blockchain Systems
  • Carbon Neutral Manufacturing Leadership

The synergy between HK-FIGURE and POSCO will unlock multi-trillion-dollar value creation through technological integration and sustainable global infrastructure development.

2. Strategic Synergy Framework (English)

Steel remains the backbone of civilization. By combining HK-FIGURE’s innovation engine with POSCO’s industrial backbone, we create a hybrid industrial-intelligence corporation.
